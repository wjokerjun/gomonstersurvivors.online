About Monster Survivors

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival action game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Test your skills, collect power-ups, and see how long you can last in this challenging adventure.

Key Features

  • Endless waves of unique monsters
  • Multiple character classes with special abilities
  • Upgradable weapons and power-ups
  • Challenging boss encounters

How To Play

Movement

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.

Attack

Click or tap to attack monsters. Some weapons attack automatically!

Power-Ups

Collect gems from defeated monsters to level up and enhance your abilities.

Game Tips

Monster Survivors gets increasingly difficult as time passes. Here are some tips to help you survive longer:

  • Focus on movement and dodging enemy attacks
  • Prioritize health and defensive upgrades early on
  • Don't get cornered - always leave yourself an escape route
  • Some weapons are better against certain types of monsters
  • Experiment with different character classes to find your playstyle
